About Me

Hi, I am a student currently studying Business Management with a goal of creating and starting a project that can turn into an enterprise

When I was young, I always had a fascination on novelty through reading documentaries of famous inventors such as Henry Ford and Thomas Edison. You may be thinking, then why did I choose business as my diploma?
Although many people recognize them as inventors, I perceived them to not only be genius inventors but world-class entrepreneurs as I believe to become the spearhead of new innovation, you first need to understand the world of business to sell your idea to the world.
Hence of my philosophies I follow in life that I feel that would help me attain my goal is that we should all focus on the experience we gain or the things we learn throughout our working lives. As I feel that the experience gained from learning new skills and working in new environments is of the up most importance in growing as a person.... and that I feel is much more valuable than money can ever buy.
